Civilization 6 Evolved is a total conversion mod of Civilization 6, in which unit upgrade paths and technologies change.
- More City Center Buildings
Added 4 more Ancient Era City Center Buildings, including Mural, Storage, and Hunting Camp.
Romans now get Mural instead of Monument at the start of the game.
Monument now requires Masonry.
Added Public School: Industrial Era City Center Building, unlocked with Scientific Theory, to provide more Science.
